Drupal Restaurant

Everything you need to build Restaurant websites: Menu management

Menu Management

A powerful managment system for creating menus, organizing menus, and uploading pictures. Support for menu types and prices included.

Reservation System

Manage your reservations easily from one dashboard. View all reservations for today, this week or this month.

Opentable

Integrates with Opentable: reservation form, badges and ratings.

Drag and Drop

Every page is customizable using a simple drag and drop ui.

Responsive theme

Works on all your devices. Bootstrap and bootswatch included.

Locations

Manage multiple restaurant locations from one site. Separate menus, address and opening hours for each location.

Events and Calendar

Create and manage events for your restaurant. View events on a calendar by date, week or month.

Blog

The distribution comes with a blog system that you can easily customize.

Widgets

Maps, address, opening hours and contact forms.

Custom look and feel

Easily customize and colorize your website's theme.
